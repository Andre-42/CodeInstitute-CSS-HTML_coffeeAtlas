How long does it take to make a cup of coffee?

I guess you would say: "A couple of minutes at my local coffee house." What if I told you it takes about a year from a blossuming coffee tree until you have your roasted coffee beans in the coffee mashine to brew your coffee. Making coffee is a lengthy process:

  1. growing the actual tree... up to 6 years
  2. flowering, seed growth and maturation... up to 10 months
  3. harvesting and drying the seeds... up to a month or two
  4. shipping / storage
  5. roasting... about 15-20 min
  6. brewing... 5 mins tops

From Flower to Seed

It is a long way from a blossuming coffee flower to the ripe fruit. Over ten months the seed undergoes multiple changes to develop the basis for the roasted coffee flavours we love so much.
